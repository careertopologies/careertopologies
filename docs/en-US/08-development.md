# Development and Training Process

[![Back](https://img.shields.io/badge/BACK-documentation-blue?style=for-the-badge)](./README.md)

Professional development is a continuous journey that requires intentionality, structure, and organizational support. This document establishes practices and processes to promote sustainable growth of professionals within the Career Topologies framework.

## Development Principles

### 1. Individual Ownership
The professional is the **main responsible** for their development.

### 2. Shared Responsibility
Managers and organization must provide support, resources, and opportunities.

### 3. Continuous Development
Learning is **continuous and incremental**: 70% practical, 20% social, 10% formal.

### 4. Focus on Impact
Development should result in **measurable impact**.

## Development Tools

### Individual Development Plan (IDP)
Structured document mapping career goals and actions to achieve them.

### Structured 1:1s
Weekly or biweekly meetings focusing on growth and feedback.

### 360Â° Feedback
Collecting feedback from multiple sources annually or before promotions.

### Mentorships
Career, technical, or leadership mentoring (6-12 months).

### Shadowing and Pair Work
Learning by observing or working alongside experienced professionals.

### Stretch Assignments
Projects or responsibilities slightly above current level.

### Training and Courses
Internal workshops, external courses, certifications, conferences.

## Formal Processes

### Performance Evaluation Cycle
Semi-annual or annual assessment against level expectations.

### Promotion Calibration
Review of promotion cases by multiple managers for consistency.

### Track Transition
Process for moving between IC and management tracks.

### Onboarding Program
30-90 day program for new hires.
